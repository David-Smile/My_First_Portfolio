Our History

TechCon began in 2015 as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts in San Francisco. What started as a 200-person meetup has grown into one of the world's premier technology conferences, attracting over 10,000 attendees annually.

Over the years, we've hosted groundbreaking discussions, launched revolutionary products, and created a global community of innovators and thought leaders.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to bridge the gap between emerging technologies and practical implementation. We believe in:

  • Fostering innovation through collaboration
  • Making technology accessible to everyone
  • Creating meaningful connections in the tech community
  • Shaping the future of technology through knowledge sharing
